From My Kitchen Nightmare to GenieChef: An Accidental App

Let's be real: I'm not a chef, and I'm definitely not a programmer. I'm just someone who was tired of staring into the fridge, wondering what to cook.

GenieChef started as a desperate attempt to solve my own daily dilemma. I was struggling to come up with easy, tasty recipes (especially keto ones!), and I thought: "There has to be a better way than eating scrambled eggs for the third day in a row."

So, I decided to build a tiny helper for myself. I had no idea what I was doing, but I kept tinkering. And a funny thing happened... my little kitchen helper grew up.

Now, it's become GenieChef‚Äîa personal AI cooking assistant that feels a bit like magic. You tell it what you have, and it gives you a recipe. No stress, no fancy skills required.

And here's my secret: This app was built by someone who can barely boil water. If it works for me, it will definitely work for you!
